Vivint

Proximity

Proximity is visible and applied in every page of the website. From the header to the footer. Everything is not clustered together and well dispersed for the design and the content with in the website. With the proximity property it helps any user navigate the website easliy with out any issues of getting lost.


Red Bubble

Alignment

For Red Bubble you will notice that all products are sold in catagories and not all placed in one spot. Each image is aligned and evenly dispersed within the website nothing out of line or next to each other. The website is definatly be easy to navigate and not confusing to find what you are looking for.


Microsoft

White Space

White space in the microsoft website is used with in the images that are presenting the products. With the use of white space within the website it is effective for any viewer to see their latest products or promotions. Its a great method to use white spaces within the website to get the user's attention
